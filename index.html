About

Builder. Researcher. Leader.

I'm a Systems Engineering & Design student at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign, passionate about solving real-world problems at the intersection of engineering, technology, and healthcare.

My flagship project, INHALYZE, is a smart inhaler prototype engineered to improve respiratory health management for Asthma and COPD patients — selected as a finalist at India's prestigious IRIS National Fair.

Beyond building products, I've led robotics education initiatives reaching hundreds of thousands of students, published engineering research, and mentored peers from underserved communities.
